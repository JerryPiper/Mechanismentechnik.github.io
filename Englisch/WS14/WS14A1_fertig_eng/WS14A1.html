Solution:

1. the degree of freedom for the whole system

  • In the first part of the task is asked for the total degree of freedom FFF. This is calculated according to Grübler with the following formula:
F=3 (n−1)−b1−2 b2F=3\,(n-1)-b_1-2\,b_2F=3(n−1)−b1​−2b2​
  • nnn is the number of links, b1b_{1}b1​ is the number of the monovalent and b2b_{2}b2​ is the number for the divalent joints.
  • The number of links is composed of 0, 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5. The third and the fifth link are represented as a surface because it has more than two link joints.
  • We don't have a movable bearing in this mechanism b1=0b_{1} = 0b1​=0.
  • For the bivalent joints we have a number of b2=7b_{2}=7b2​=7. For that the two fixed bearings and the other five joints are counted.
  • Our result would be:
F=3 (6−1)−0−2⋅7=1F=3\,(6-1)-0-2\cdot 7 = 1F=3(6−1)−0−2⋅7=1

2. the number of meshes

  • For the rule of meshes, the following equation applies
m=b1+b2−n+1m=b_1+b_2-n+1m=b1​+b2​−n+1
  • The relation of the variables from subtask 1 also apply here. So, the number of meshes would be:
m=0+7−6+1=2m=0+7-6+1 = 2m=0+7−6+1=2
