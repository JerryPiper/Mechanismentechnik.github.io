Exercise 4:

Two-layers synthesis

For the two layers of the illustrated arrow, the frame point A0A_0A0​ and the link point BBB of a four-bar linkage are to be determined.The link point should lie on the arrow straight line and the frame point on line bbb.

Draw the complete four-bar joint in both positions.

Solution:

  • First connect the two arrowheads with a straight line (D1→D2D_1 \rightarrow D_2D1​→D2​) and record the first perpendicular bisector. The same is done with the starting points of the arrows (C1→C2C_1 \rightarrow C_2C1​→C2​) , where you also draw the second perpendicular bisector.
  • Our point P is the intersection of the two perpendicular bisectors, around which we will turn later to find the points which are asked in the exercise.

Procedure for determining the frame point A0A_0A0​:

  • For this purpose, the pole angle φ0\varphi_0φ0​ must first be determined. For this we need a leg sa\color{#5858FA}s_asa​, which passes through the point P and through the limb point of the first link length C1C_1C1​.
  • Since our leg passes through the point C1C_1C1​, we also take the associated perpendicular bisector from the distance C1C_1C1​ to C2C_2C2​, because this is where our center line ma\color{#5858FA}m_ama​ runs.

  • Now we turn our two straight lines with the angle φ0\varphi_0φ0​ , until our leg sa\color{#5858FA}s_asa​ passes through the point AAA.
  • Our point A0A_0A0​ must be on the midline ma\color{#5858FA}m_ama​
  • Since we can deduce from the task that our frame point must also lie on the line bbb, we also record this where the lines ma\color{#5858FA}m_ama​ and bbb intersect.

Procedure for Determining Limb Point BBB:

  • Here we first again determine the pole angle, here called φ1\varphi_1φ1​.
  • The leg sb\color{#01DF01}s_bsb​ passes through the points P and D1D_1D1​. Appropriately, we take midline mb\color{#01DF01}m_bmb​ between D1D_1D1​ to D2D_2D2​. The included angle of these two lines is our φ1\varphi_1φ1​.

  • We turn the two lines around point P.
  • Analogous to the first pole angle, the frame point B0B_0B0​ must also lie on the center line mb\color{#01DF01}m_bmb​ and the link point BBB somewhere on the leg sb\color{#01DF01}s_bsb​.
  • Due to the condition in the task that the point BBB must lie on an arrow line, we can draw this, at the point where the two lines intersect.
